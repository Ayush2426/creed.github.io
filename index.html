Hey, Welcome to CREED! Established in 2019 , TEAM CREED claims to create an ecosystem of gamers , creators , influencers , coaches and managers . We focus on filling the wide gaps of this industry between gamers and audiences by creating unique solutions to the existing and potential problems . We are trying to create an atmosphere of competition that empowers upcoming talents to become their best . As audience is the backbone of the industry we take care of our engaging and interactive audience which supports gaming. Stepping into more E - SPORTS titles , we provide a platform which gives free and fair opportunity for everybody to grind and practice. We also have our management services for anybody who would like to partner with us . E - SPORTS coverage is another aspect. In order to make our audience aware of the latest happenings in the gaming world , we create unique and original gaming content that would help our audiences have a better understanding of the gaming industry.
